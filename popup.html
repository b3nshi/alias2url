Alias2URL

    How to use it?

    1. Open a new tab and visit the URL you want to create an Alias
    2. Open this popup, and fill in the alias you want to use and click "Add"
    3. Open a new tab and write your new alias prefixing it with "#". Ie: #ALIAS
    4. Press "enter" and you should be redirected.
